Digital Manufacturing Research Initiative

Short presentation of the project

Within the framework of the project Digital Manufacturing Research Initiative (DIGIMARI) the University of Applied Sciences (Technische Hochschule Mittelhessen) with financial support from the Federal Ministry of Education and Research analysis the possibility of enhancement of digital transformation in companies. The partner network of the project consist of the universities in Gießen and Marburg, the Regionalmanagement Mittelhessen and further an association of 30 companies called the "Smart Electronic Factory". Also the Japanese German Business Association (DJW) supports the project.
Within the 1 ½ year lasting BMBF campaign "The Future of Work" connections between potential Japanese companies in the Kansai region will be gained. This region could be called "industrial heart of japan" because it contains beside some large companies many small and medium-sized production enterprises. Furthermore, there are also a huge number of education and research institutes located there, which also belong to our target group. Through the cross-national partnership digital businesses will be developed, innovations in digitalization of the manufacturing chain will be made and also know-how will be transferred.

Research Network of Central Hessen

In the course of the project Digimari powerful economical and entrepreneurial companies grouped to the Research Network of Central Hessen. This leads to a wide and ideal line-up for the digitization in the industrial production.
partners:

Planned measures

Following measurements are in progress:

  • Marketing activities
    • Beside talking directly to potencial cooperation partners, far-reaching online channels will be implemented (website and social media). Two delegation trips to Kansai are also planned where we will perform a roadshow and a symposium. All these actions serve to enhance the reaching of the project.
  • Central service desk
    • A central desk for the project DIGIMARI will be organized. It will serve as a firsthand contact (service desk) which will deal with all activities around the project.


Service range and objective

Objective of the project DIGIMARI is to capitalize the Research Network of Central Hessen in japan to initialize cooperation projects in the field digitalization of small and medium-sized enterprises.

  • Partnerships
    • Building joined research projects and collaborations to operate comparable research especially on the field of organization and business models.


  • Test platforms
    • Provide demonstration and research platforms. Building joined analog research platforms in japan and further a cross-national research platform


  • Know-How transfer
    • Implement demonstrators of digitization to test concepts. Exchange of practical experiences and concepts.
